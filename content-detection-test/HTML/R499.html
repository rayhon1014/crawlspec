NFL Fantasy LIVE podcast (11/6/12)

NFL.com Staff | Tags: NFL Fantasy Live podcast,

NFL Fantasy Live – November 6, 2012 Hour 1

This week on the “NFL Fantasy Live” podcast, Jason Smith, Michael Fabiano and Elliot Harrison celebrate the election season as they welcome in this term’s candidates for the President of Fantasy Football, Adam Rank and Akbar Gbajabiamila. Also, the guys expand of the constant wave of fantasy football woes facing the Philadelphia Eagles.

NFL Fantasy Live – November 6, 2012 Hour 2

In the final hour of the “NFL Fantasy Live” podcast, Smith, Fabiano and Harrison head to the Cantina to answer your fantasy inquiries. Also, the guys play “Pick-A-Player,” and what has Michael Fabiano so worked up that he might start breaking things?
