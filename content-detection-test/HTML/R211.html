    Energy XXI Bermuda Breaks Above 200-Day Moving Average - Bullish for EXXI

    Energy Stock Channel, Contributor
    comments, called-out
    + Comment now
    + Comment now

    In trading on Thursday, shares of Energy XXI Bermuda Ltd (NASD: EXXI) crossed above their 200 day moving average of $33.71, changing hands as high as $34.41 per share. Energy XXI Bermuda Ltd shares are currently trading up about 2.7% on the day. The chart below shows the one year performance of EXXI shares, versus its 200 day moving average:

    Click here to find out which 9 other stocks recently crossed above their 200 day moving average »

    Looking at the chart above, EXXI’s low point in its 52 week range is $26.20 per share, with $39.65 as the 52 week high point — that compares with a last trade of $34.03.
